Our office primarily deals with civil law, economic, public procurement, company law and copyright issues, real estate matters, document drafting, as well as media law and inheritance matters. In addition to all of this, we participate in case studying in the field of public administration and carry out efficiency studies in several areas of economic life. Our office is awarded the Middle Cross of the Order of Merit of the Hungarian Republic. Volunteering is highly important for us. In voluntary work, we represent the Munka-Kör Foundation for the Hearing Impaired and the GTTSz NGOs in litigation and out-of-court cases.
